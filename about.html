About Us

Kicks of the Past is a vintage football shop dedicated to preserving the rich history of the beautiful game. We specialize in sourcing, collecting, and selling authentic retro football jerseys, classic boots, and other rare memorabilia. Our passion is to connect true fans and collectors with iconic pieces of football heritage.

Our Mission

Our mission is to keep football history alive by offering fans and collectors access to rare and unique vintage items. We aim to inspire passion for the game by providing authentic memorabilia that represents unforgettable moments in football.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading destination for football fans and collectors worldwide, creating a community where the love of the sport and its history continues to grow and be celebrated for generations to come.
